Macrophage recognition

Welcome to this macrophage recognition model, based on computer vision algorithm VGG-16.

To use the model, simply upload your image (making sure the image is a square and that the macrophage is clearly visible).
The algorithm will then classify your picture into one of 8 categories, namely:

  • Basophil
  • Erythroblast
  • Eosinophil
  • Immature granulocytes
  • Lymphocyte
  • Monocyte
  • Neutrophil
  • Platelet.
